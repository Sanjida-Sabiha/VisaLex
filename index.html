About VisaLex - Immigration Lawyers in UK

Why Our Law Firm?

VisaLex specializes in Business Immigration services and custom HR systems tailored for the UK. Our OISC (Office of the Immigration Services Commissioner) approved immigration advisers provide extensive support to UK employers. This includes assistance with Sponsorship Licence Applications, requesting and allocating Certificates of Sponsorship (CoS), offering Sponsorship Management System (SMS) user training, and aiding in Visa Applications.
